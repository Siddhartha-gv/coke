About Coca-Cola

Discover the story behind the world's most iconic beverage brand and our commitment to refreshing the world.

Our Story

Coca-Cola was invented in 1886 by pharmacist John Stith Pemberton in Atlanta, Georgia. The name "Coca-Cola" was suggested by Pemberton's bookkeeper, Frank Mason Robinson, who also designed the now-famous logo in flowing script.

From its humble beginnings, Coca-Cola has grown to become one of the most recognized brands in the world, serving customers in more than 200 countries with a portfolio of more than 200 brands.

Today, Coca-Cola continues to refresh the world and make a difference, bringing people together through its iconic beverages.

Our Mission

Our mission is to refresh the world in mind, body, and spirit. To inspire moments of optimism and happiness through our brands and actions. To create value and make a difference.

We strive to be a total beverage company, offering a diverse portfolio of quality beverages that anticipate and satisfy people's desires and needs.

We are committed to sustainable business practices and giving back to the communities we serve, ensuring a better shared future for our planet.

Our Values

Passion

We are committed to our brands and the refreshment they provide. We have the courage to pursue our dreams and the conviction to realize them.

Sustainability

We are dedicated to creating a more sustainable business and better shared future that makes a difference in people's lives, communities, and our planet.

Community

We believe in the power of coming together. We foster an inclusive environment where everyone's contributions are valued and respected.

Innovation

We seek new ideas and better ways of doing things. We embrace change and continuously strive to improve our products and services.
